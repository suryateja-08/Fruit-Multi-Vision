Discover Global Fruits

FruitMultiVision is an innovative web app designed to identify and classify lesser-known fruits from various countries, tailored specifically for Indian clients. By harnessing the power of deep learning technology, we aim to uncover unique fruit varieties that are often overlooked, fostering a greater appreciation for global biodiversity. This app serves as a valuable resource for fruit enthusiasts, chefs, and nutritionists alike, offering insights into the flavors, uses, and benefits of these rare fruits.

Key Features

AI-Powered Classification

Utilizes advanced deep learning models to accurately identify and categorize over 40 varieties of lesser-known fruits from around the world.

Cultural Insights

Provides information about the origins, uses, and nutritional benefits of each fruit, enhancing users' understanding and appreciation of global fruit diversity.

User-Friendly Experience

Designed with an intuitive interface that allows users to easily upload images and receive rapid classification results.With seamless navigation and quick processing.
